About us

Who we are

We are the UK ‘s leading model support service. We are not an agency as we do not match models with work but instead we are the first port of call for anyone looking to break into the industry but are unsure of where to start. Alongside our ongoing advice we offer practical help to allow you to find out if you show model potential and can even help you to build your very own professional model portfolio.

Why choose us?

With over 18 years of experience, we have helped aspiring models of all ages achieve their dream of becoming a model.
